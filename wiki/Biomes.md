# Biomes

Overview of the biome system and resource distribution.

---

## What are Biomes?

Biomes determine what resources miners can extract at each location.

---

## Biome Types

| Biome | Primary Resources |
|-------|-------------------|
| Plains | Iron, Coal |
| Desert | Copper, Sand |
| Mountains | Iron, Stone, Rare ores |
| Forest | Wood, Coal |

---

## Resource Probability

Each biome has a probability table for resources.

Miners at different locations may produce different items.

---

## Spawn Area

The spawn area contains guaranteed biomes for initial resources.
